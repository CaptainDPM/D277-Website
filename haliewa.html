Northern Surf Town on Oahu

Haleiwa is a charming town located on the North Shore of Oahu, one of the Hawaiian Islands. Known for its laid-back atmosphere, stunning natural beauty, and world-class surfing conditions, Haleiwa offers visitors a unique blend of Hawaiian culture and outdoor adventure. The town serves as a gateway to some of the best surfing spots in the world, attracting surfers from around the globe who come to ride the legendary waves of the North Shore.

Side note:

Haleiwa is home to one of the best shrimp food trucks on the island... Giovanni's!


In Haleiwa, you'll find a picturesque main street lined with local shops, art galleries, and eateries. The town has a rustic, old-school vibe with its plantation-style buildings, colorful storefronts, and a sense of history. It's a popular spot for visitors to explore and enjoy a leisurely stroll while soaking up the local atmosphere. Haleiwa is also famous for its shave ice, a beloved Hawaiian treat made with finely shaved ice and topped with an array of delicious syrups.

Beyond the town itself, Haleiwa is surrounded by breathtaking natural wonders. The North Shore's coastline is dotted with stunning beaches, such as Waimea Bay and Sunset Beach, which offer not only great surf but also opportunities for swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing. Nearby, the Waimea Valley boasts a lush botanical garden and a majestic waterfall, providing a tranquil escape into nature. Haleiwa is a must-visit destination for those seeking a blend of laid-back beach culture, outdoor adventure, and the authentic Hawaiian experience.
